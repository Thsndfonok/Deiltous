Philosophy

“Simplicity is not the absence of complexity; it is the result of mastering it.”

Deiltous believes constraints sharpen clarity. Black and white force composition, spacing, and motion to carry meaning. Every pixel is accountable.

Principles

  • Hierarchy first, decoration last.
  • One idea per screen.
  • Motion reinforces—not competes.
  • Accessibility is non‑negotiable.

FAQ

Do you work only in black & white?
For this series—yes. Constraints help us design with intention. Color can join later when it adds meaning.
What about performance?
We avoid heavy libraries, use semantic HTML, compress assets, and keep animations GPU‑friendly.
How do we start?
Email us with context and goals. We’ll propose a minimal path to value.
